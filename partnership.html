Isgec - Hitachi Partnership

ISGEC Heavy Engineering is a distinguished industrial powerhouse committed to delivering excellence in engineering and manufacturing solutions across multiple sectors. Our expertise encompasses the design, fabrication, and commissioning of critical equipment and infrastructure for industries such as power, sugar, cement, and steel. With a strong foundation built on innovation and quality, ISGEC leverages advanced technology and skilled craftsmanship to meet the unique needs of clients worldwide. Beyond manufacturing, we emphasize strategic partnerships and collaborative ventures, enabling us to expand our capabilities and deliver comprehensive, end-to-end solutions. Our unwavering dedication to customer satisfaction and sustainable practices positions ISGEC as a trusted partner in driving industrial progress and empowering businesses globally.

Hitachi Zosen Corporation, Japan

Hitachi Zosen Corporation, Japan, established in 1881 and headquartered in Osaka, is a leading engineering and industrial company known for its expertise in infrastructure, environmental solutions, and industrial machinery. Originally founded as Osaka Iron Works by British entrepreneur Edward H. Hunter, Hitachi Zosen has evolved into a global corporation with more than 12,000 employees. The company specializes in designing, building, and maintaining a variety of projects, including energy-from-waste plants, desalination facilities, water and sewage treatment plants, marine diesel engines, and advanced industrial machinery. In recent years, Hitachi Zosen has become a pioneer in clean energy and carbon-neutral technologies, focusing on sustainable solutions such as hydrogen production and innovative waste management. Guided by a philosophy of creating value for society through technology and sincerity, Hitachi Zosen continues to contribute to sustainable development and environmental responsibility across Asia, Europe, North America, and the Middle East.

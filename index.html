Company Overview


At ISGEC Heavy Engineering Ltd., we leverage over 90 years of expertise to engineer and manufacture diversified industrial solutions—ranging from process equipment and boilers to hydraulic presses—delivering sustainable, state-of-the-art products to customers in 92 countries worldwide. Our commitment to engineering excellence drives innovation and reliable project execution, empowering industries across sectors to achieve operational distinction. With expansive manufacturing facilities and advanced technology partnerships, ISGEC stands as a trusted global engineering partner dedicated to quality and customer success.


ISGEC Heavy Engineering specializes in delivering innovative engineering and manufacturing solutions primarily for the power, sugar, cement, steel, and infrastructure sectors. With deep expertise in designing and fabricating critical process equipment, boilers, pressure vessels, and heavy machinery, ISGEC ensures high-quality, reliable products that meet stringent industry standards. Recognizing the importance of technological advancement and sustainability, we continuously invest in research and development and strategic partnerships to enhance our capabilities. Our comprehensive approach combines superior engineering, customized solutions, and after-sales support to empower industries globally, driving operational excellence and long-term growth for our clients.
